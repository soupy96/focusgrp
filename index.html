Who we are

At FocusGRP, our mission is sustainable results. We are a non-partisan firm that works with all political parties, levels of government and stakeholders. We bring a simple approach to solve complex issues. We do ‘deep dive’ research to understand the challenge. We build relationships and leverage our expertise to deliver realistic, reasonable and measurable results.

Our service offerings fall into three categories: government relations, research, and public relations. We’re a new company staffed by highly seasoned experts. Our strength lies in our attention to detail and diverse experience.

Government Relations

Government Relations

We've worked extensively with government and in government as political staff. We understand what and who influences government decision-making. With a view from the inside, we know the political structure and the role of Cabinet Ministers and Ministers’ Office staff, Members of Parliament, Mayors and City Councils. We understand the challenges inherent with bureaucracy, and the influence of community associations and business groups. We're about working collaboratively and seeking reasonable solutions to complex issues.

Research

Research

Research is the core of all that we do. It provides full confidence that we have a 360 degree understanding of the issue. Research informs the solution and eliminates unwelcome surprises. It's the foundation that informs everything we do. With comprehensive, targeted research, we can build a successful strategy that’s measurable by key performance indicators. We also provide research as a stand-alone service.

Public Relations

Public Relations

Our expertise is the key to unlocking your full potential as you work to inspire and move your audience. We help you protect and promote your brand and your reputation. We offer communications planning, media relations training, issues management and crisis communications solutions. We can help you increase staff satisfaction and productivity through our employee communications expertise. Finally, we provide a full range of writing services.
