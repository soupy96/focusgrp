Doing Business with Those Who Share Our Values

Laura Blondeau
VP, Communications
March 03, 2020



What’s old is new again, and what’s new is the broadening expression of ethics beyond corporate giving and feel-good PR campaigns. Increasingly, it includes making conscious and conscientious choices about who to do business with, based on similar values.

The advent of the printing press in the mid-1400s resulted in the mass production of books and ultimately, much higher literacy rates. Reading about peoples’ experiences and feelings led folks to demonstrate increased empathetic behaviour toward others.

Today, the online world helps us understand the bigger picture and the global challenges of people and planet. Social media connects us directly with people everywhere, and modern transportation can take us almost anywhere within hours.

This heightened awareness is having broad implications in corporate decision-making. Increasingly, customers, clients, employees, and leadership have expectations of how companies should do business and with whom.

At FocusGRP, we’ve built our corporate values in a manner that ensures our client-focused philosophy AND big-picture sustainability goals are entirely compatible.

As one of our corporate values, we’ve defined sustainability as People, Planet & Prosperity: generating winning solutions in a way that promotes the social, environmental and economic health of people, companies, and communities for generations to come.

Our approach guides us in our decision-making every step of the way. It includes how we do business and with whom, as well as the projects and products we champion and promote.

One such product is dynaCERT inc. | Carbon Emission Reduction Technology (HydraGEN) which reduces fuel consumption by up to 19% and carbon emissions by up to 50% for diesel engines. This green technology saves money for companies and reduces their carbon footprint.

This year, dynaCERT was awarded as the number one ranked company across all sectors on the 2020 TSX Venture 50.

One of our principals here at FocusGRP is a dynaCERT board member and we’re pleased to promote the company to our clients and in fact to everyone at every opportunity.

We’ll continue to work with, plug, promote and seek partnerships with new, innovative companies and entrepreneurs that have a product or service that considers big picture sustainability.

Talk soon!
